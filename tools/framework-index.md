# Framework Index

## Service Leadership

Category: Central doctrine

Use when the work involves leadership, growth, contribution, responsibility, culture, or institutional development.

## String Leadership Theory

Category: Leadership development

Use when the work involves maturity, self-awareness, leadership dimensions, management growth, or developing others.

## EntrSL / ELEM

Category: Research and learning systems

Use when the work involves student engagement, entrepreneurship education, motivation, learning culture, or service-learning.

## HYOH

Category: Career agency

Use when the work involves career clarity, self-leadership, personal direction, ownership, or professional growth.

## AIRe / Culture Snapshot

Category: Organizational culture

Use when the work involves recognition, appreciation, incentives, morale, retention, management, or team culture.

AIRe is credited to Vantage Circle. EntrLabs is currently doing research utilizing this framework.

## Shared Doctrine

All frameworks point back to the same center:

```text
Strength Through Service
```
