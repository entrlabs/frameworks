# Service Leadership

**Growth designed around contribution.**

Service Leadership is the central doctrine behind EntrLabs.

It combines servant leadership, developmental thinking, learning culture, and organizational design into a practical system for helping people and institutions grow through service.

## Core Equation

```text
Servant Leadership + String Leadership Theory = Service Leadership
```

## Core Claim

People, teams, and institutions develop best when growth is connected to contribution.

## What It Solves

Service Leadership responds to several common failures:

- Leadership reduced to charisma
- Culture reduced to slogans
- Growth reduced to personal ambition
- Service reduced to passivity
- Development separated from responsibility

## Practical Questions

- What contribution is needed here?
- What capability must grow to make that contribution possible?
- What system reinforces the contribution?
- What responsibility needs to become clearer?
- Who else must be developed?

## Applications

- Founder leadership
- Management development
- Service-learning design
- Nonprofit leadership
- Career agency
- Team culture
- Institutional development
